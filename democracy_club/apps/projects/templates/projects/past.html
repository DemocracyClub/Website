TakePart London

{% filter markdown %} On the run up to the 2016 EU referendum we [plotted the electoral register for the whole of London](https://democracyclub.org.uk/blog/2016/06/03/8-out-10-cats-have-registered-vote-your-area/) against population estimates. From this we made a heatmap and tool for looking up registration rates by postcode. This included pictures of the area taken from [Scenic Or Not](http://scenicornot.datasciencelab.co.uk/) and passed through a [DeepDream](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DeepDream) generator. [View the code on GitHub](https://github.com/DemocracyClub/TakePart.london) {% endfilter %}
