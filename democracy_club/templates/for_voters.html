Where Do I Vote?

One of the most common questions from voters on election day is “where do I vote?” our polling station finder has the answer! Democracy Club is the only organisation that aggregates and cleans all the polling station data across the UK. Find your polling station wheredoivote.co.uk.

Who Can I Vote For?

Another common question from voters is “who can I vote for?” with the help of thousands of volunteers we collect candidate data and make it available to all voters ahead of every election. Find your candidates at whocanivotefor.co.uk.
